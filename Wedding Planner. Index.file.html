Your Wedding OS
Welcome. Let’s get you started.

This planner is your complete wedding command centre — budget, guests, vendors, seating, mood board, honeymoon and more — all in one private file on your device.

📋
Planning tab

Budget categories, vendor profiles with payment tracking, guest list with RSVP and meal dropdowns, planning timeline, day schedule, and full ceremony builder with printable order of service.

🍴
Meal options

Add your menu options in the Meal Options section first. They then appear as dropdowns on every guest row and a bar chart shows the live breakdown of how many guests chose each dish.

🍴
Table plan tab

Drag tables around a room canvas. Click any seat to assign a guest. Colour-coded by RSVP. Assignments sync back to the guest list automatically. Use the Edit button on any guest chip to update their details.

✨
Mood board tab

Upload inspiration images filtered by category. Build your palette and style statement. Print or share as a formatted text summary with your florist, photographer, or family.

✈️
Honeymoon tab

Log all travel details and work through a smart packing checklist with a progress bar. Add your own items. Syncs with your export.

🏛
Venue comparison

Add up to 6 venues and score them across 8 criteria. The top scorer gets a badge. A comparison table appears automatically once you have 2 or more venues.

💾 Saving your data

Everything saves automatically as you type. Use the Export button regularly to download a backup. To move to another device, export on one and import on the other.

💡 Top tips
Define meal options first. Add your menu choices before collecting RSVPs.
Booked a vendor? Set their status to Booked and their quote auto-appears in your budget.
Edit any guest. Click the Edit button on any row to update name, RSVP, meal, table, dietary, email and phone.
Export often. After any major session hit Export and save to Google Drive or Dropbox.
